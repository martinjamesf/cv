Transforming Enterprise Communications

by James Martin, Senior User Experience Designer

As a Senior User Experience Designer at American Family Insurance, I led a major project to redesign our companyâ€™s communications platform, driving significant improvements in engagement and productivity across the organization.

Challenge

With 83% of our workforce operating remotely (100% during the pandemic), our communications team faced the challenge of unifying our messaging platform. Competing tools caused confusion and inefficiency, making it difficult to deliver consistent and effective communication across the organization.

Solution

We developed "Compass," a centralized platform designed to streamline communication, deliver consistent messaging, and foster a sense of belonging across the organization. The solution included:

  • Personalized Navigation: Developed role-specific, action-oriented global navigation.
  • Content Accessibility: Implemented expandable blocks for announcements and recent content.
  • Streamlined Search: Simplified the search functionality to minimize reliance on multiple engines.
  • Customizable Dashboards: Enabled creation and sharing of configurable dashboards.

Results

The redesigned platform significantly boosted engagement and productivity by delivering personalized user experiences, streamlining content access, and enhancing search capabilities. The platform continues to evolve through ongoing user feedback and adaptation to meet changing organizational needs.
